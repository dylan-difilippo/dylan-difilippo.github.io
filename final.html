Final Project - River Rush

RiverRush Beta v1.20

River Rush is a game written in Javascript by Brad St. Jean and myself, Dylan DiFilippo. In our game, the player takes the role of a tiny fish in a gigantic river filled to the brim with enemies of ranging sizes. Eat opposing fish that are smaller than you to grow in size and conquer the river! There are also collectable coins that will net you alternate skins and backgrounds! Have fun!
